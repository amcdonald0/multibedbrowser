What Is The Multi-Bed Browser?

The Multi-Bed Browser is a modern web-based platform designed to simplify how you upload, view, and manage multiple files in one place. Using your browser, you can easily access your files without needing to install extra software. Simple, efficient, and user-friendly.

Key Features

Easy File Management

Upload, organize, and manage your genomic data files with ease. Our intuitive interface makes it simple to handle multiple BED files simultaneously.

Advanced Visualization

View and analyze your genomic data with powerful visualization tools. Compare multiple tracks, zoom into regions of interest, and export your findings.

Collaborative Research

Share your data and findings with colleagues worldwide. Our platform supports real-time collaboration, making it easier than ever to work together on genomic research projects.
